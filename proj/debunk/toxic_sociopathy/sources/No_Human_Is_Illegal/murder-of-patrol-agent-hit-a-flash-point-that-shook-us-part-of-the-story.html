December 22, 199812:00 AM ET
Heard on Morning Edition

McCaughey's Septuplets

Audio will be available later today.

Joyce Russell of member station WOI reports on the progress of the McCaughey septuplets of Carlisle, Iowa, who are now 13 months old. Many volunteers, mostly from churches, have helped take care of the children. Bobbi and Kenni McCaughey, the children's parents, say they hope to reduce the number of non-family helpers substantially in the near future.
